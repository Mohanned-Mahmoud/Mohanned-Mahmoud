### 🧠 About Me  
I am an **Artificial Intelligence Engineer** and researcher passionate about designing intelligent, end-to-end systems that bridge cutting-edge research and real-world impact. I am currently completing my Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence at Nile University.

Beyond the code, I bring a unique perspective to my work: I am a former Taekwondo player and a passionate visual artist who loves drawing with pencil and wooden colors. This blend of martial arts discipline and artistic creativity drives my approach to building visually stunning, highly optimized software.

🎯 **Mission:** Building AI that learns, understands, and seamlessly integrates into human-centric applications.  
🌍 **Languages:** English (Fluent) | Arabic (Native)  
📍 **Location:** Cairo, Egypt

### 🚀 What I Do
- 🧩 **AI & Deep Learning** – Training, fine-tuning, and deploying LLMs, Transformers, and CNNs using TensorFlow and PyTorch.
- 🌐 **Full-Stack Development** – Architecting scalable web and mobile ecosystems using Next.js, React, Flutter, Node.js, and FastAPI.
- 👁️ **Computer Vision & NLP** – Developing real-time object detection (YOLO), emotion recognition, and advanced seq2seq translation pipelines.
- ⚙️ **Data Engineering** – Designing ETL pipelines, Star Schemas, and Medallion Architectures for massive datasets.
- 🥽 **Spatial Computing (VR/XR)** – Creating immersive, physics-driven educational environments using Unity, C#, and Meta XR SDK.
